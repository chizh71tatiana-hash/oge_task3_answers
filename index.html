Вариант 1

Books

↑ Наверх
Задание

Task 3. You are to give a talk about books. You will have to start in 1.5 minutes and will speak for not more than 2 minutes (10–12 sentences).

Remember to say:
  • where you and your friends can get books to read
  • whether you prefer a traditional book or an audiobook, why
  • why people read less nowadays than they used to
  • what your attitude to reading is

    • You have to talk continuously.

    Ответ в виде темы

    I am going to give a talk about books.

    I think books are important in our life.

    My friends and I can get books in different places.

    We can borrow them from a library, buy them in a bookshop or download them from the Internet.

    Some people also listen to audiobooks on their phones.

    I prefer a traditional book to an audiobook because I can read it at my own speed.

    But audiobooks are useful when people are travelling or walking.

    I think people read less nowadays because they spend a lot of time on phones.

    They often watch videos instead of reading.

    In my opinion, reading is a very useful habit.

    It helps us become smarter and speak better.

    That's all I wanted to say about my topic.
